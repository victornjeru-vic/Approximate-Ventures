Approximate Ventures Ltd – Company Description (Public View)

Approximate Ventures Ltd is a professional Kenyan support services company dedicated to helping hospitals, organizations, and businesses streamline their operations. We specialize in health insurance claims management, patient admission & discharge coordination, documentation support, and general administrative services.

With a focus on accuracy, speed, and transparency, we ensure that hospitals and clients experience smooth workflows, minimized claim rejections, and timely communication. Our team is committed to reliability and excellent service delivery, enabling organizations to focus on their core work while we handle the operational demands.

At the heart of Approximate Ventures is a commitment to professionalism, integrity, and efficiency — ensuring every client receives dependable support and consistent results.
